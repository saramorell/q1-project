About

Hello! Thank you for taking a look around my site. I am a photographer and designer living outside of Boulder, Colorado with my husband, son, and a small flock of backyard chickens. I love to photograph food, and the people and places connected to it.

As the marketing director for a Boulder area restaurant group, which included a world-renowned teahouse and online tea company, I had the opportunity to photograph a LOT of tea. I loved it, and yes, I drank a lot of tea and still do.

With six other restaurants and a organic farm in the group I also shot food, buildings, chefs, farm dinners, workshops, chickens and goats. Additionally I was the in-house graphic designer, website manager, resident tech geek, and on-demand crafter (chalkboard signs, holiday decor etc.).
